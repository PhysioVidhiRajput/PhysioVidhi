Meet Dr. Vidhi Rajput (PT)

Welcome to my professional space! I am Dr. Vidhi Rajput (PT), a recently qualified Physiotherapist, having successfully completed my Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT). My journey through physiotherapy has shaped me into a compassionate and dedicated healthcare professional with a passion for helping people restore movement, overcome pain, and improve their quality of life. I believe in combining scientific knowledge with a patient-centered approach to deliver effective care.

This website reflects my commitment to continuous learning, growth, and service as I step into the professional world of physiotherapy, eager to make a positive difference in people’s lives.

Our Services

Sports Injury Rehab

Specialized programs for athletes of all levels to recover from injuries, prevent future issues, and enhance performance.

Orthopedic Care

Effective treatment for joint pain, back and neck pain, arthritis, and post-surgical recovery.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including mobilization and manipulation to reduce pain and improve mobility.

Neurological Rehab

Rehabilitation for conditions like stroke, Parkinson's, and multiple sclerosis to improve function and independence.

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Gentle and effective care for children with developmental delays, congenital disorders, and injuries.

Home Visit Consultations

Professional physiotherapy services delivered in the comfort and convenience of your own home.
